Ramen Recipe

Description

Ramen is a wonderful food from the great country of Japan.

This bowl of brothy noodles are sure to keep you warm during the winter nights.

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Ramen broth
  • Egg
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Cook ramen noodles according to package.
  2. Boil eggs until jammy on the inside.
  3. Place ramen noodles, boiled eggs, and chopped green onion into the broth.
  4. Eat!
