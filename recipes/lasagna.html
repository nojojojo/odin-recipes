Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a wonderful food from the great country of Italy.

Layers of broad noodle are placed in a dish between dollops of sauce and fresh mozzerella.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package.
  2. Place one noodle on baking dish, then place pasta sauce, and finally the mozzerella cheese.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, then place in oven for 40 minutes.
  4. Eat!
