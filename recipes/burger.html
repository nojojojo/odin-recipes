Burger Recipe

Description

Burgers are a wonderful food from the great country of America.

Between two buns is a hamburger patty, lettuce, tomato, and cheese. Yum!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns
  • Ground beef
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into patties and cook medium-high on a pan until medium-rare.
  2. Assemble the burger by adding patty, cheese, lettuce, tomato, and ketchup on a bun.
  3. Eat!
